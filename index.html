About Me

Chikezie Emmanuel Odoh is a driven young Nigerian born on the 3rd of October in Mararaba, Nasarawa State. He is currently a third-year student of Computer Science and Statistics at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka.

Roots & Family

Raised in Abuja, Chikezie completed both his primary and secondary education there. He is the proud firstborn son of Mr. and Mrs. Ugochukwu Odoh and comes from a family of five children.
